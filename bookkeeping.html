BOOKKEEPING APPLICATION

This is a current work in process with very limited CSS in place at the moment.

This is a custom web-based bookkeeping solution that I built from the ground up to support Farmhouse on the Pond's accounting needs. Due to the unique business model, none of the existing bookkeeping programs met all of the business's needs. It tracks accounting, expenses, sales (both of individual and farmers markets) as well as allowing for real time pricing of recipes and ingredients. There is extensive tracking and custom reports can be generated on any data point that is tracked. Sample reports include how many of a recipe is sold, what a customer orders and how often, when eggs are bought, how many and how much they cost each time, etc.
