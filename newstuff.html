    About

    I've been lucky to have a career that I'm passionate about for the last fifteen years, working for a company that creates and sells lasertag hardware and software all over the world. I have worn many hats, but for the past several years my focus has been on development. I am motivated by my relationship with clients and my love of the game to create fun, interactive user experiences. I build and maintain websites, databases, and perform server maintenance and updates. When I'm not working, I volunteer my time with The North Ridge, a local Star Wars group that appears throughout the Buffalo area at charity events, most notably Star Wars Night with the Buffalo Bisons. You might know me as Director Krennic.
